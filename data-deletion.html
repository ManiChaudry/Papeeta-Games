User Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

This page applies to all mobile games published by Papeeta Games, unless otherwise stated.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to request deletion of data associated with your use of any Papeeta Games application, please contact us by email:

Email:
papeetagames@gmail.com

To help us process your request more efficiently, please include:

  • The name of the game (for example, VoidStrike: Space Shooter or Find the Difference)
  • Your device platform (Android)
  • A brief statement requesting deletion of your data

How We Process Your Request

After receiving a valid request, we will review it and, where applicable, take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize data associated with your use of our applications.

Because our games rely on trusted third-party services, certain data is processed by those providers rather than directly by Papeeta Games. Where applicable, deletion or anonymization will be handled in accordance with the policies and technical capabilities of those providers.

Third-Party Services

Our games may use one or more of the following services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP)
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Remote Config

Data We Do Not Store

Papeeta Games does not operate user account servers and does not directly store personal user profiles, passwords, or payment information.

All purchases are securely processed by Google Play. Payment card information and other financial information are never stored by Papeeta Games.

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited period where required for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security purposes
  • Compliance with applicable laws
  • Backup and disaster recovery processes maintained by our service providers

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding access, correction, deletion, or restriction of the processing of your personal information, subject to applicable laws.

We will review all valid requests and, where applicable, work with our third-party service providers to process them in accordance with applicable laws and their privacy policies.
